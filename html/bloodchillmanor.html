Bloodchill Manor Landscape Fix

A patch to fix landscape issues when using Bloodchill Manor from the Creation Club alongside other mods.

Bloodchill Manor makes landscape changes in cells that a lot of other mods revert to vanilla, even if they don't otherwise use the location. This patch simply applies Bloodchill Manor's landscape edits again lower down your load order.

Installation

To install Bloodchill Manor Landscape Fix, you first need to download it. The mod is available on NexusMods.

NexusMods

You can install it with a mod manager of your choice, or extract the zip to Skyrim's Data folder.

This mod should be placed close to the end of your load order.

Uninstall

To uninstall Bloodchill Manor Landscape Fix, delete the mod from Skyrim's Data folder or from your mod manager.
